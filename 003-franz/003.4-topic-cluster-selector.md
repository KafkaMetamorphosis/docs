# Topic Cluster Selector

A topic definition says what is the shape of a topic given the name it has and its configuration, but it also needs something to say where it should be in. For instance:

You have 30 kafka clusters, where 10 of them are standard, 10 are high volume and 10 are fast clusters.  

Eg.1: 
A team uses a single topic that has a high throughput so it should be placed in high volume tiered clusters. The question is, which of those clusters should the actual topic?

Eg.2: 
A team uses many topics that has a low volume, but has a really low latency and high avaiability needs. So the team decided to have the topic in many clusters to make sure if a kafka cluster fail, all the feature won't be affected.
