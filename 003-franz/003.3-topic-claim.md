# Topic Claim

The topic claim is the declaration of the state of a topic to a single cluster defined by the topic definition, in other words is the expectation of a topic.

### Topic Claim

| `GET` | `/api/v0/clusters/:cluster-name/claims?status=<status>` | Return the claims with all (not specified) or specific state. |
| `PUT` | `/api/v0/clusters/:cluster-name/claims/:claim-id/change-state` | Set claim state. There is some allowed transition. |
